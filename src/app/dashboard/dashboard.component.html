12 Dog Breeds That Only Have White Coats

Large or small, these white dogs stand out

1.Samoyed

                  HEIGHT: 19 to 24 inches

                  WEIGHT: 35 to 65 pounds

                  COAT  : Thick double coat

                  COLORS: White, cream, and biscuit

                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 12 to 14 years

2.Maltese

                  HEIGHT: 8 to 10 inches

                  WEIGHT: 4 to 7 pounds
                
                  COAT: White fur, long and silky

                  COLOR: Dark eyes and black nose
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 12 to 15 years

3.West Highland White Terrier

                  HEIGHT: 10 to 11 inches

                  WEIGHT: 13 to 20 pounds
                
                  COAT: Medium-length fur white coat

                  COLOR: White
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 12 to 16 years

4.American Eskimo

                  HEIGHT: 9 to 12 inches  

                  WEIGHT: 6 to 10 pounds
                
                  COAT: Stand-off double coat

                  COLOR: All-white
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 13 to 15 years

5.Bichon Frise

                  HEIGHT: 9 to 12 inches

                  WEIGHT: 7 to 12 pounds
                
                  COAT: Resembling a cotton ball 

                  COLOR: Fluffy and curly white hair
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 12 to 15 years

6.Great Pyrenees

                  HEIGHT: 25 to 32 inches

                  WEIGHT: 85 pounds and up
                
                  COAT: Thick double coat

                  COLOR: White with gray or red
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 10 to 12 years

7.Coton de Tulear

                  HEIGHT: 9 to 11 inches

                  WEIGHT: 8 to 15 pounds

                  COAT: Fluffy white coat

                  COLOR: White

                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 14 to 16 years

8.Bolognese

                  HEIGHT: 10 to 12 inches

                  WEIGHT: 5.5 to 9 pounds

                  COAT: Long, curly white hair 

                  COLOR: A black nose and dark eyes

                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 12 to 14 years

9.Dogo Argentino

 
                  HEIGHT: 23 to 27 inches

                  WEIGHT: 80 to 100 pounds
                
                  COAT: Smooth and short coat

                  COLOR: All-white 
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 9 to 15 years

10.Japanese Spitz

                  HEIGHT: 12 to 15 inches 

                  WEIGHT: 10 to 25 pounds
                
                  COAT: Straight stand-off outer coat 

                  COLOR: Dark eyes, nose, and lips
                
                  LIFE EXPECTANCY: 12 to 14 years

11.Komondor

                HEIGHT: 26 to 28 inches

                WEIGHT: 80 pounds and up
                
                COAT AND COLOR: White corded coat
                
                LIFE EXPECTANCY: 10 to 12 years

12.Kuvasz

                HEIGHT: 26 to 30 inches

                WEIGHT: 70 to 115 pounds
                
                COAT: Thick double coat

                COLOR: Solid white
                
                LIFE EXPECTANCY: 10 to 12 years
